FAQs

How are you today?

Pretty great actually—thanks so much for asking! We hope you’re having a great day as well.

Help—I lost my password!

Don’t worry, we’ve got you!

1. Head here to reset your password
2. Enter your email address (the one you used at checkout)
3. Check your email inbox for further instructions

Once I've subscribed, how do I access the library?

It’s as easy as 1, 2, 3:
1. Sign in to your account
2. Click on My Learning Dashboard
3. Scroll down to the “My Optimism Library” section and select a video!

You’ll have access as long as you’re subscribed. 

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

If you decide The Optimism Library isn’t right for you, you can get a full refund within 14 days of purchase. Simply contact us here—no questions asked.

You can cancel your subscription at any time to prevent automatic renewal for the following year. Cancellation can be done through your account settings.

Does Simon teach these classes?

You bet! Find Simon’s wisdom nuggets in the “Learn with Simon” section of the Library and you can access all of his bestselling courses, including The Art of Presenting.

But that’s not all—your subscription now includes those exciting new quarterly live Q&A sessions where you can interact with Simon directly! And when Simon’s catching his breath, our world-leading experts and Optimist Instructors step in to deliver those monthly live sessions and other fantastic content. It’s like having the whole optimism dream team in your corner!

Are the classes live?

Most of the content in The Optimism Library consists of on-demand recordings that you can access anytime. However, your subscription now includes new quarterly live Q&A sessions with Simon and monthly sessions with leadership experts over Zoom. We’ll notify you about upcoming dates for these live events, and you can always find the schedule in your dashboard.

For additional live experiences with your team or company, reach out to our B2B experiences team at https://business.simonsinek.com/contact-us/.

Are all of your courses in the library?

Great news! We’ve gone all in! Your subscription now includes Simon’s complete course collection. That means everything from The Art of Presenting to Find Your WHY is now at your fingertips—no separate purchases needed. Plus, we’re still adding fresh content every month to our library of 125+ existing lessons. Consider this your all-access pass to the world of optimistic leadership!

Can I watch these classes on mobile?

Yes! We recommend only doing so over Wi-Fi, unless you have an unlimited data plan.

Can I buy multiple subscriptions for my company?

You can buy up to 50 subscriptions through our website, or you can request a bulk order by writing us at [email protected]. 

Still have a question or need help with your subscription?

You can use the Optimism Library Contact Form and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can!

How do I access the live Q&A sessions with Simon?

Once you’re logged into your dashboard, you’ll find the schedule for upcoming live sessions, including Simon’s quarterly Q&As. We’ll also send email notifications before each session with the Zoom link and details so that you can RSVP. Can’t make a live session? Don’t worry! Recordings will be available in your library afterward.

Can I ask questions during the live sessions?

Absolutely! That’s what makes these sessions special. You can submit questions in advance or ask them live during the Q&A portion. It’s your chance to get Simon’s perspective on the problems you’re trying to solve and the dreams you wish to achieve.
