How do these Team-Building programs work?

Theyre super easy!

A single log-in is all you need for your entire team of up to 10 participants. Simply follow the interactive module which includes videos, discussion questions, optional games and get-to-know-yous, and a few surprises along the way.

The programs are self-led just by following the module. If youd like to inquire about having one of our Optimist Instructors facilitate team-building programs for your organization, get in touch with our Experiences Team.

How many people can participate?

A single Team-Building Program purchase is intended to accommodate groups of up to 10. For much larger groups, please contact sales.

How do Team-Building Programs work for organizations of different sizes?

Weve test-driven our Team-Building Programs with groups of all sizes, from tight-knit teams to sprawling organizations. Heres what we found:

For small teams or businesses:
Got less than 10 participants? One program has got you covered.

For medium to large organizations:
Please reach out to our sales team for more information on a tailored package just for you.

Weve seen larger organizations arrange programs within and across different departments how it is configured can lead to some of the best serendipity across your team.

What if I don't have a team?

Weve still got you covered with a live online class on The Infinite Game.

How long do I have access to the content?

You have access to the on-demand content for one year from date of purchase.

Is this a live experience?

The content is all on-demand and is easily able to be completed by your whole team by simply following the module and on-screen prompts.
