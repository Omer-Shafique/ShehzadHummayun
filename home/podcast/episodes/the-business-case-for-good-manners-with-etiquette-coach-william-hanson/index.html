The Business Case for Good Manners
with etiquette coach William Hanson

Share with a friend

Good manners arent just about being polite theyre about making other people feel seen.

William Hanson is one of the worlds leading etiquette coaches. Hes advised royalty, CEOs, and television personalities on how to communicate with clarity, confidence, and grace. But his mission goes far beyond fine dining or proper handshakes.

In todays fast-paced, informal world, William argues that etiquette isnt outdated its essential. Whether youre trying to land a job, win over a client, or simply connect with others, good manners are your most underrated advantage.

He sat down with me to share how etiquette builds trust, why its not about snobbery or perfection, and how anyone regardless of background can learn the unspoken codes that open doors.

This is A Bit of Optimism.
